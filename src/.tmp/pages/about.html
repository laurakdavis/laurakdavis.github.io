Who am I?

I’m a writer with a passion for helping the most innovative software brands in the game build their audience and generate new business. I’m fascinated with digital marketing and data analytics and recognize the power that regular, exclusive content can have on a brand’s growth.

About Me

Hi, I'm Laura.

I’m a Boston-based freelance writer (though I’m originally from the Jersey Shore). I graduated from Boston University with a Bachelor of Science in Mass Communication and a specialization in Public Relations. My interests include iced coffee, reading books and listening to podcasts about personal development, my two fluffy orange cats, and Taylor Swift.

My Background

I’ve been a writer since I could hold a pencil, but I became interested in writing about digital marketing specifically in 2017 when I joined the team at Esri, one of the leading B2B GIS software companies in the world. In my role, I worked across three teams: paid advertising, search engine optimization and social media. I helped manage a huge advertising budget, optimized the website and ads for best results in search and generated gripping social media content across Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn. I quickly became obsessed with the incredible work done by SaaS companies and it became my mission to use the power of content to drive traffic and generate leads for cutting-edge software brands.
