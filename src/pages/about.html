Who am I?

I’m a writer with a passion for helping the most innovative software brands in the game build their audience and generate new business. I’m fascinated with digital marketing and data analytics and harnessing the power of regular, exclusive content.

Hi, I'm Laura.

I’m a Boston-based freelance writer (though I’m originally from the Jersey Shore). I graduated from Boston University with a Bachelor of Science in Mass Communication and a specialization in Public Relations. My interests include iced coffee, reading books and listening to podcasts about personal development, my two fluffy orange cats, and Taylor Swift (haters gonna hate).

My Background

I’ve been a writer since I could hold a pencil, but I became interested in writing about digital marketing specifically in 2017 when I joined the team at Esri, one of the leading B2B GIS software companies in the world. In my role, I worked across three teams: paid advertising, search engine optimization and social media. I helped manage a $2 million advertising budget, optimized the website and ads for best results in search and generated gripping social media content across Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn. I quickly became obsessed with the incredible work done by SaaS companies. Now, it’s my mission to use the power of content to drive traffic and generate leads for cutting-edge software brands.

What does the process look like?

Get to Know Me

Check out my latest work and get a feel for the type of content that lights me up and my writing style. Think we’d be a good fit?


I’ll Get to Know You

Reach out using the contact form. Make sure to include your company name and any important info like your branding pain points and goals. From there, I’ll reach out to schedule a consultation call. We’ll talk in detail about your goals for your brand and what type of content you’re looking for. Still think we’re a good fit?


Onboarding

I’ll send you a proposal and rate quote within 2 business days. You’ll review the proposal options and choose the ones you like. I’ll send you a contract including deadlines we’ve agreed on and an invoice for a 50% deposit. Once that’s signed and paid, we’re in business. I’ll ask you for any additional resources I need to complete the job effectively, like introductions to internal team members for interviews or specific data you want me to incorporate.


Writing & Editing

Now for the good stuff! I’ll get to work researching and writing. When the first draft is done, I’ll share it with you in Google Docs. You will then go through the draft and provide your feedback and questions. I’ll edit based on your suggestions and let you know when it’s ready. We will repeat this part up to two times (as two revisions are included).


Finishing Up

Once you sign off on the final product, I’ll send you an invoice for the remainder of the bill. Then, I’ll send you a brief survey so I can collect any feedback you want to share about us working together. If you enjoyed our time working together and have other content needs to address, we can talk about next steps to an ongoing arrangement.
